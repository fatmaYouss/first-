Hospital, an institution that is built, staffed, and equipped for the diagnosis of disease;

for the treatment;both medical and surgical,

of the sick and the injured; and for their housing during this process.

The modern hospital also often serves as a centre for investigation and for teaching.

what is a hospital ?

the hospital is that treatment institution responsible for providing health care to patients through specialized medical staff and medical devices, which from a vital part of the public health services .

the hospital includes all medical specialties , and it is required to be equipped with the following facilities:

erating rooms,recovery rooms,and rooms for patients, public and private

some of them also have outpatient clinics.

it includes laboratories,radiology and emergency departments,and a highly qualified staff of doctors and nurses work there.

Department and Specialties

The hospital is equipped with the latest medical devices,and the hospital includes ateam of distinguished doctors in the medical field ,in addition to the highly skilled and experienced nursing staff ,and the hospital is managed by an automated system specialized in the medical field.

Department Of General Surgery


The department of neurology offers a number of treatments and techniques for treating tumors in the head

,spine ,skull,and cartilage surgeries of the spine

Department Of Obstetrics and Gynecology


The Obstetrics and Gynecology Section is equipped with the best medical techniques
ensuring the highest care for any medical emergencies
that may occur during labor or postpartum period. The section includes also special surgeries rooms.

The Anesthesiology Department


The Anesthesiology Department The Anesthesiology Department includes operating and recovery rooms.
The department also provides a large number of safe and effective medical services.

The Dental Section

The Dental Section offers the best treatment in coordination between experiences of dental consultants and specialists from various fields,
including oral medicine, orthodontics, dental implants, endodontics, restorative dentistry, periodontology,
oral and maxillofacial surgery, prosthodontics, pedodontics, and esthetic dentistry.

The Orthopedic Section

The Orthopedic Section offers its services through a group of highly qualified Orthopedic consultants
and several specialized clinics that cover the following services: Traumatology Pediatric orthopedic surgery
Arthroscopy Hand surgery Foot surgery Spinal surgery Joint replacement Osteomyelitis

OUTPATIENT PHARMACY

OUTPATIENT PHARMACY: Outpatient pharmacy dispenses medications to outpatient clinics.
One of the most important pharmacy objectives is to educate patients and instruct them on
how to use medications in order to obtain best possible health outcomes.
